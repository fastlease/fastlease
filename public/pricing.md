# Pricing — FastLease

Toronto condo leasing brokerage operated by Property.ca Inc. Brokerage. Contingent on signed lease — no upfront, listing, photography, or walk-away fees.

## Standard fee (signed day 1–21)

- **Price:** One month's rent
- **Trigger:** Signed lease with a qualified tenant inside 21 days from list date
- **Includes:** Listing on property.ca + condos.ca (featured), MLS, Realtor.ca, Zumper, Kijiji and 9 other syndication platforms, professional photography, all showings hosted, five-layer tenant screening, lease paperwork
- **Payable:** On signed lease only

## Discounted fee (signed day 22+)

- **Price:** 79% of one month's rent (automatic 21% reduction)
- **Trigger:** Automatic if a qualified tenant is not signed by day 21
- **Includes:** Same service — no reduction in marketing, showings, or screening
- **Suspension:** Guarantee is suspended if the owner lists more than 5% above FastLease's recommended price range (disclosed in writing before sign)

## What's not charged

- No listing fee
- No photography fee
- No early-termination fee
- No "premium placement" surcharge
- No fee if no lease is signed
